Must-Visit Tourist Spots in Jember

Jember is home to a variety of tourist attractions, from natural landscapes to historic sites. Here are some must-visit spots:

Papuma Beach

Known for its beautiful white sands and clear blue waters. It’s an ideal spot for a relaxing day by the sea.

Watu Ulo Beach

Named after the rock formations that resemble a giant serpent. It’s a place rich in local myths and stunning views.

Sukorambi Botanical Garden

A serene garden boasting a wide range of flora. Perfect for a peaceful walk and nature photography.
